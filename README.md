# Outbreak ZeroHour

A 3D zombie survival game developed in Unity during my internship at Akiyam Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Outbreak ZeroHour focuses on creating an immersive survival experience through atmospheric environments, AI-driven zombie encounters, responsive third-person gameplay, and continuous gameplay optimization. During my internship, I contributed to multiple aspects of the project, including environment development, player controller improvements, AI integration, bug fixing, and overall gameplay enhancement.

---

## Internship Information

**Role:** Unity Game Developer Intern  
**Company:** Akiyam Solutions Pvt. Ltd.  
**Duration:** November 2025 – May 2026

---

## My Contributions

### Player Controller Integration & Enhancement
- Integrated the JU TPS (Third Person Shooter) Player Controller provided by the company.
- Improved player movement responsiveness and overall gameplay feel.
- Refined movement mechanics to create a smoother third-person experience.

### AI Enemy Integration
- Worked with AI Zombie Enemies provided by the company.
- Integrated enemy AI into gameplay scenarios.
- Configured enemy behavior and interactions with the player.

### Environment Design
- Designed and refined environments to support the game's survival-horror atmosphere.
- Enhanced scene composition and environmental storytelling.
- Improved the visual presentation of gameplay areas.

### Atmospheric Visual Effects
- Added visual filters and environmental effects to strengthen the game's immersive atmosphere.
- Enhanced mood and ambience through post-processing and scene adjustments.

### Gameplay Optimization & Bug Fixing
- Identified and resolved gameplay-related bugs.
- Improved in-game functionality and player experience.
- Assisted in optimizing game systems for smoother performance.

### Team Collaboration
- Worked alongside the development team to deliver project milestones.
- Contributed to maintaining project quality and development progress.
